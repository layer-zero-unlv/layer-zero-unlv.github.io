Information and Cyber Security at UNLV

We are a group of students passionate about learning
how to protect personal and others digital information.

Thank you for visiting our homepage. We are excited for the 2019-2020 school year.

We are currently working on our meeting schedule. Check our calendar periodically for updates.

Meetings will resume in the Fall after recruiting and orientation. Recruiting will start first two weeks of Fall Semester (Aug 27-Sept 10). Our recruiting officers will be visiting classes and distributing flyers. There will be a challenge for potential members to solve (don't worry, it supposed to be fun!). We will then precede to conduct interviews to get to know each potential member better.

Those who are interested please click here and send us a message with your first name, last name, and school email address. We will put you on a priority recruiting list and contact you directly when the recruiting session starts.
